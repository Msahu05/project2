About the Developers

HoomanMart is a fun and creative project developed by Mohit Sahu and Devraj Patel, two passionate developers from the IT branch of R C Technical Institute. Driven by curiosity and creativity, they aim to bring unique and humorous experiences to life through code.

Our Mission

To build innovative and entertaining web applications that combine design, humor, and interactivity. HoomanMart is just one step in our journey of making the web more playful and engaging.

Previous Projects

Before HoomanMart, we created an interactive quiz website that tested users' knowledge while keeping them entertained. It was our first step toward designing user-centric platforms with personality.

What We Offer

  • Web projects with a twist of humor and creativity.
  • Clean and functional user interfaces.
  • Passion-driven development that reflects individuality and teamwork.
